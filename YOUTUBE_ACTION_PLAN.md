### Task 1.4: ToolStack - Hook Restructure
**Owner:** Content/creative team
**Time:** 2-3 hours
**Due:** 3/25

**Current Problem:** Superlative claims ("Faster than 10 developers") get clicks but zero engagement.

**Hook Restructuring Exercise:**

Current: "This AI Assistant Codes Faster Than 10 Senior Developers!"
- Problem: Skeptical viewers, no emotional stake
- Fix: "I used this AI tool for a week and replaced a $5K/month contractor. Here's how."
  - Specific benefit (saved $5K)
  - Emotional stake (lost job/time)
  - Actionable (here's how = people watch to learn)

Current: Generic AI tool reviews
- Fix: "5 AI tools I tested for 30 days—which one is worth your money?"
  - Comparison (5 tools)
  - Personal testing (credible)
  - Question hook (engages viewer decision-making)

**Deliverable:** 3-5 rewritten hooks that follow pattern:
- [Specific benefit] + [Emotional stake] + [Actionable]

**Test Protocol:**
1. Create 1 video with new hook format
2. Publish 3/25
3. Measure engagement for 48 hours
4. If engagement > 2%, approve full restructuring and scale
5. If engagement < 1%, iterate again

**Success metric:** New hooks drafted by 3/25, first test video published by 3/26

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: StoryVault 2x daily sprint burns out team
**Mitigation:**
- Use templated production pipeline (script → render → upload)
- Consider outsourcing 50% of video production
- Rotate team weekly to prevent burnout

### Risk: Cold Cases runs out of unique story ideas
**Mitigation:**
- Expand source pool: Reddit + news + historical archives
- Create series (unusual tools, unusual locations, etc.)
- User-submitted stories (builds community)

### Risk: ToolStack hooks don't improve engagement
**Mitigation:**
- Pivot to interview format (creator spotlight)
- Consider merging with Cold Cases (unusual AI use cases)
- Or sunset if engagement stays < 1%

### Risk: CryptoFlow pivot doesn't gain traction
**Mitigation:**
- Personal finance format has proven success (less saturated than crypto)
- If pivot fails, sunset channel and reallocate resources
- 60-day prove-it period before major investment

This action plan assumes:
