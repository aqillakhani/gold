HIGH-IMPACT QUALITY ISSUES

4. REPETITIVE FORMULA (All 24 scripts)
   ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

   reddit_stories:    Problem → Escalation → Payoff → CTA
   crypto_finance:    Claim → Data → Doomsday → Disclaimer
   ai_tools:          Tested → Amazed → Comparison → Affiliate link
   true_crime:        "What if?" → Victim → Investigation → Breakthrough → Moral

   Impact: Viewers recognize formula by video 2-3, engagement drops

   Fix: Vary structure occasionally (non-linear narrative, debate format, etc.)

5. WEAK CLOSING CALLS-TO-ACTION (All 24 scripts)
   ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

   Lazy patterns:
   • "What would you do in this situation? Drop your thoughts below" (appears 4x)
   • "Follow for more workplace drama stories" (appears 3x)
   • "This is not financial advice" (appears 5x in crypto scripts)
   • "Some links may be affiliate links" (appears in ALL 6 ai_tools)

   Problem: Generic, obligation-based CTAs don't create engagement

   Better approach: Specific questions that invite real discussion
   Example: Instead of "What would you do?" try "Would you risk $5M? Why or why not?"

6. FILLER LANGUAGE AND PADDING (All niches)
   ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

   Example: "The AI doesn't just write code - it understands context, suggests
            optimizations, and even fixes bugs as it goes."
   → "Understands context" and "fixes bugs" are same concept
   → Artificially stretches runtime

   Solution: 20% tighter scripts (250w → 200w average)
   → Every sentence must add new info or emotional resonance

7. INCONSISTENT AUTHENTICITY
   ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
